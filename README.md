## 📖 About

Cultiv8 Solutions is an AI-powered smart agriculture platform that combines the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), and Data Analytics to help farmers monitor crops, improve productivity, and reduce water and resource waste.

Our mission is to make agriculture more intelligent, sustainable, and accessible by providing real-time monitoring and automated decision-making systems.

Initially, Cultiv8 is focused on Pakistan, with the vision of expanding globally.

# 🌍 Problem Statement

Traditional farming relies heavily on manual inspection and fixed irrigation schedules. This often leads to:

- Water wastage
- Low crop yield
- Late disease detection
- Poor monitoring
- Increased farming costs

Cultiv8 solves these challenges by using AI and IoT to provide real-time insights and automate crop care.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Cultiv8 continuously collects environmental data using IoT sensors.

The collected data is analyzed using Artificial Intelligence to:

- Detect unhealthy conditions
- Predict plant diseases
- Recommend actions
- Automate irrigation
- Improve productivity
