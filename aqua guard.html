Why Cultiv8

Agriculture is the backbone of entire economies — it deserves better tools

Farmers continue to face unpredictable weather, inefficient resource management, and plant disease, often without the data to act early. We believe technology can close that gap.

Cultiv8 is an AgriTech startup combining Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things to transform how plants are monitored and cared for — turning raw sensor data into real-time insight, proactive alerts, and informed decisions.

The result: healthier crops, higher productivity, and more sustainable farming practices, delivered through hardware and software built to be affordable and reliable from day one.

🌿
Tech Stack

A robust core architecture

  • ✓Processor Core: Wi-Fi enabled microcontrollers (ESP32 / ESP8266) handling signal logic and secure data pipelines.
  • ✓Sensor Network: Dual-pronged conductive soil moisture probes paired with DHT11 atmospheric sensors for temperature and humidity.
  • ✓Fluid Infrastructure: Low-voltage submersible pumps switched through isolated micro-relays for safe, reliable irrigation.
  • ✓Cloud Layer: Encrypted telemetry syncs sensor data to the dashboard and AI models in near real time.
